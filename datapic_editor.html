How to change the Image

1) URL:Upload your background image to your school's server (check with your teacher for details). Replace the 'background-image'URL with the path to your picture. When done, copy the code and paste it into your schools website to display your data pic.

2) Local computer: Insert the path to your file as a 'background-image' url eg. file://PATH-TO/test.png. To publish your work, press the 'preview' button, take a screenshot of the preview, crop the pic and save it as a png. The png file can then we uploaded to your school website.
